Prize Giving Criteria


Sportsperson Selection Criteria

  • Two awards Sportswoman & Sportsman.
  • Award based on Current Year Sporting Achievements.
  • Senior student in year 12 or 13.
  • Represent/selection school in various sports.
  • Represent/selection district in sports.
  • Represent/selection country in sports.
  • Ownership to represent school or country in sports not offered in school.
  • Leadership role like class captain, school prefect, student council member.
  • Discipline like positive results in late-coming/attendance/incident report.

Academic Awards Y9-Y13

  • 1st, 2nd, & 3rd Positions from Each Form.
  • Top 5 Students at Each Syndicate Level, Y9, Y10, Y11, Y12, & Y13.
  • Best Subject Awards at Each Syndicate Level, Y9, Y10, Y11, Y12, & Y13.

External Exam Awards

  • 2018 Highest Subject Scorers Y10, Y12 & Y13
  • 2018 Overall Highest Scorer Y10, Y12, & Y13

Subject Prizes Y13

  • Best Arts Student
  • Best Science Student
  • Best Commerce Student
  • Best Technical Science Student

Dux


Highest Mark from all Five Subjects in Y13 from term 1, term 2, term III trial, annual & final exams


Dux Award Criteria

  • A year 13 student.
  • All marks from term I, term II & III trial exams & annual exam will be taken into account.
  • Average marks from the above trial & exam will be considered.
  • The student with highest mark to one decimal place will be the dux of the school.
  • All five subjects will be considered.
