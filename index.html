Monogram


The badge design is a shield signifying with education one can protect its dignity. The banner at the bottom is a sign of excellence. The lighted candle represents vision and success. The book represents wisdom and knowledge. The writing in latin at the bottom, means “to light & truth”. Light blue colour signifies the location of our school in a beautiful country. Yellow colour represents brightness and fairness. The black border is associated with strengths of our prestigious school.
